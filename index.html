The Vessel

Experience the Clermont chain of lakes on a vessel designed for both performance and comfort. SV Verity is a 1989 Catalina 25 with a wing keel shoal draft and a hull speed of 6 knots powered by an original 1989 nissan longshaft 2 stroke outboard. She is hull number 889 out of 900 which makes her the 11th to last ever made from the total production. She was made for coastal cruising but has and always will be a lake boat. Whether we are catching the afternoon breeze for a lesson or enjoying a steady sunset charter, our boat is kept to the highest standards of safety and maintenance.

Perfect for small groups, couples and individual instruction, it offers a stable and responsive ride, making it the ideal platform for beginners to learn the ropes and for experienced sailors to enjoy a refined day on the water.
