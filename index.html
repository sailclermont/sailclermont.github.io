About the Captain

With over 15,000 nautical miles logged in both offshore and coastal yacht deliveries worldwide, We bring a level of expertise to Clermont that is truly unique. After years of navigating a bit of the worlds oceans and living aboard in St. Petersburg, I’ve returned to my hometown with my wife Madison to share the joy of sailing on our local lakes.

Whether you're looking for a relaxing sunset charter with Captain Madison or serious technical instruction on basic keelboat sailing with Captain Austin, you're learning from a captain who has found out he still hasnt seen it all. Our mission is to build a vibrant local sailing community right here on the Clermont chain of lakes.
